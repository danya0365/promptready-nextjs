Step 1: Install Ollama
Go to ollama.com/download and install it on your machine.
This is what allows your laptop to run AI models locally.
Takes about 2 minutes.

---

Step 2: Download Gemma 4
Download Gemma 4 26B A4B directly to your device.
This model is both capable and fast making it the perfect choice for running local AI agents.
You can actually skip this step if you want because Step 3 handles it automatically.

---

Step 3: Launch OpenClaw With Gemma 4

```
ollama launch openclaw --model gemma4:26b
```

Run this single command and OpenClaw installs itself and launches with Gemma 4 as the backend automatically.
That is it. Your own private AI is now live on your laptop.
